▣ Reading Your Sheet

A quick rundown before you get started \u2014 you can reopen this anytime from the (?) button up top.
Attributes
BODY \u2014 raw strength and toughness. Raises Max HP and how much cyberware you can carry.
REFLEXES \u2014 speed and reaction time. Drives your combat edge.
TECH \u2014 technical aptitude. Also raises cyberware capacity, alongside BODY.
INTELLIGENCE \u2014 hacking and netrunning aptitude.
COOL \u2014 composure under pressure and resistance to intimidation.
EMPATHY \u2014 social aptitude and connection to other people.
Vitals
HP \u2014 your current health. You edit this yourself to track damage and healing.
Max HP \u2014 auto-calculated from BODY and Level, the ceiling HP can't exceed.
Armor \u2014 damage reduction, auto-calculated from any armor-granting cyberware you have installed.
CAP \u2014 cyberware capacity. How much chrome you can carry before you're maxed out, based on BODY and TECH.
Humanity \u2014 starts at 100 and drops as you install more invasive cyberware. Get low enough and you risk cyberpsychosis.
Cyberdeck & Progress
RAM \u2014 your cyberdeck's processing pool for running quickhacks. Only shows once a deck is installed.
Level \u2014 rises automatically with XP and unlocks higher-tier gear.
Street Cred \u2014 your reputation with fixers, earned from completed gigs.
Eddies \u2014 your money. Everything in every shop costs eddies.
